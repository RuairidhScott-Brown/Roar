# Target Design Specification

- Type & Role (Mission) 
- Number of crew 
- Payload 
- Range 
- Flight altitude & speed 
- Ceilings: absolute, service, combat
- Turn rates: aerobatic, fighter, speed at which the turns can be performed
- G-limits 
- Runway Requirements: surface, length
- Powerplant 
- R&M targets 
- Design standards 
- Cost targets
